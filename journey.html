My Birthday

Sareya Village Gopalganj, Bihar, India

Humble beginnings in a small village that shaped my values and determination.

Humble Beginnings

  • Born in Sareya, a small village in Gopalganj district of Bihar
  • Grew up in modest rural setting with values of hard work, resilience, and perseverance
  • Life was simple yet challenging, instilling determination to rise above circumstances
  • Second child and first son among six siblings
  • Education was a distant dream for most families in the village
  • Prevailing mindset prioritized survival over schooling

Village Transformation

  • Many families believed more children meant greater strength for land protection
  • Conflicts often settled with lathis rather than law
  • Gradual change in perspective - people started valuing education
  • Sareya became one of the most educated villages in the region
  • Transformation paved way for next generation's opportunities
  • Seeds of change continue to flourish with ongoing progress
