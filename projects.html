Warehouse Automation

Mondelez Bahrain Warehouse Automation

Implementation of an Automated Storage and Retrieval System (ASRS) to enhance warehouse operations, increase efficiency, and integrate with existing ERP systems.

ASRS SAP Integration Robotics WMS

✅ Project Overview

The Mondelez Bahrain Warehouse Automation project involved the design, installation, and integration of an Automated Storage and Retrieval System (ASRS) to optimize warehouse processes.

✅ System Specifications

  • 4 Conveyors for material movement
  • 2 Lifts for vertical transportation
  • 10 Floors for structured storage
  • 10 Shuttle Robots for automated retrieval
  • 32 Aisles per side (2 sides) per floor
  • Pallet capacity of up to 1000kg
